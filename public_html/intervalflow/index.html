Interval Flow

Have you ever found yourself struggling to keep track of time when trying to transition smoothly between activities? Interval Flow is designed to solve precisely that problem and transform the way you manage your time with your Apple Watch.

Imagine effortlessly transitioning from one activity to another, whether it's your breathing exercises, your mindfulness rituals, or any other tasks you have in mind. With Interval Flow, you can easily set multiple timers that will ring one after the other, guiding you seamlessly through your activities.

No more fumbling for your watch or constantly checking the time. Interval Flow ensures that your Apple Watch becomes a smart partner, helping you stay on track without any distractions. It's like having a personal timekeeper right on your wrist.

But that's not all! Interval Flow goes above and beyond with its customizable programs feature. You can create multiple programs tailored to your specific needs and preferences (watchOS 10+).

With just a few taps on your Apple Watch, you can effortlessly switch between programs and enjoy a seamless flow of activities, staying focused and productive. It's like having your very own personal trainer and time management coach, all conveniently accessible from your wrist.

So, the question is, why settle for a standard timer when you can elevate your Apple Watch experience with Interval Flow? It's a game-changer for anyone who values their time and wants to make the most of every minute.

Privacy policy

No personal data is sent to any server, as there is no server component to this app. There are neither third party server integrations, accounts, analytics, advertising, nor hidden trackers in this app. All your data is kept on your iPhone, unless you choose a cloud provider to sync or store your data. See your cloud provider's privacy policy for details on how they may handle it.
